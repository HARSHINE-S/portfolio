Digital Farm Management Portal

HTMLJavaScriptPython

Disease outbreaks in pig and poultry farms cause major economic losses due to poor biosecurity, where infections spread silently through visitors, vehicles, and daily operations. Farmers lack integrated, real-time tools for monitoring and prevention. We propose an Integrated Digital Biosecurity and Farm Management Platform that unifies key farm operations. It includes QR-based visitor and vehicle control, biosecurity checklists, health and vaccination tracking with alerts, and inspection recording. Multilingual support, activity logs, search options, and emergency reporting ensure transparency and quick response. This platform helps farmers prevent outbreaks, reduce losses, and improve livestock productivity, strengthening India’s livestock sector and supporting rural economic growth.
